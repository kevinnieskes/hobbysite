Fallout 3

The Best way to End Civilization!

Fallout is an Amazing Golden age of scifi style, post apocalyptic future. Set 200 years after a massive nuclear war with China you emerge from a Vault, an underground bunker, to explore the strange, strange wasteland. The game has a very good sense a humor, and is a first or 3rd person shooter. The first 2 games in the series were turn based town down rpgs and it stays true to this formula but having a unique taraget system called v.a.t.s. the lore of the game is extremely interesting and it is one of few games that allow you to kill everyone you meet and not fail the game.
